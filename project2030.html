Project 2030

2026 – 2030  ·  Five Pillars  ·  One Direction

Full Plan Coming Soon

The 2020–2025 Five Year Plan stabilized MSL through one of the hardest periods in the organization's history and the world. It set honest targets, reduced costs, and kept the foundation intact. That work matters and this plan builds directly on it.

Project 2030 is not a recovery plan. It's a growth plan. A five-year institutional roadmap covering membership, civic education, digital infrastructure, governance, and statewide impact; written to outlast any single administration.

Every incoming Governor will be expected to review it, report on it, and revise it where reality demands. That's what separates a plan from a promise.
