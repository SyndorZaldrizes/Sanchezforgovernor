# Sanchez | Daramola — MSL Governor & Lt. Governor 2026

Campaign website for **Nathan Sanchez Alvarado** and **Timi Daramola**, candidates for Governor and Lieutenant Governor of the Maryland Student Legislature (MSL). Built as a static site hosted on GitHub Pages.

## The Ticket

**Nathan Sanchez Alvarado** - Candidate for Governor  
Current DCP of the Maryland Student Legislature. Rebuilt his delegation from zero to 30+ members in a single semester. Works directly alongside Maryland state legislators on research across education, AI, energy, and economic issues. Developed and teaches a credit-bearing legislative drafting course, and is the architect of Project 2030; a five-year institutional roadmap for MSL.

**Timi Daramola** - Candidate for Lt. Governor  
Current VDCP of the Howard Community College delegation and serving on the Council of State Communications team. Focused on civic education, recruitment infrastructure, voter engagement, and expanding the resources and opportunities available to every MSL member. Co-architect of Project 2030.

## Platform

Big Dreams; Real Systems:

- **Education Infrastructure** *(Sanchez)* — A transferable credit course curriculum adoptable at any member institution
- **Transparency & Institutional Governance** *(Sanchez)* — Semesterly reports, appointed cabinet, succession protocol
- **Chapter Expansion & Delegation Development** *(Sanchez)* — Systematic activation of 30+ inactive campuses; target 250 at Annual Session by 2030
- **Fundraising & Financial Development** *(Sanchez)* — Grant pursuit, statewide events strategy, chapter-level Financial Development Committee
- **Civic Engagement & Town Halls** *(Sanchez)* — Delegation-hosted public forums and civic facilitation training
- **Community Engagement & Voter Drives** *(Daramola)* — Voter registration drives across all active campuses, peer-to-peer outreach training
- **Internship & Career Pipeline** *(Daramola)* — Updated internship tracker, resume resources, alumni mentorship
- **Civic Education & Video Resources** *(Daramola)* — Short-form video series on Parli Pro, bill drafting, and how MSL works

## Project 2030

Project 2030 is a five-year institutional strategic plan for MSL, building directly on the 2020–2025 Five Year Plan. It covers five pillars: Membership & Growth, Civic Education, Digital Infrastructure, Institutional Governance, and Statewide Impact. It is a governance document, not a campaign promise; designed to be reviewed, reported on, and revised by every incoming administration.

Full plan available at [project2030.html](project2030.html).

## Technical Details

- Pure HTML5 and CSS - no frameworks, no JavaScript dependencies
- Mobile-responsive layout via CSS Grid and viewport meta
- Google Fonts: Playfair Display, Source Serif 4, DM Mono
- Hosted via GitHub Pages (deploy from main branch)

## Deployment

1. Clone or fork this repository
2. Enable GitHub Pages under Settings → Pages → Deploy from branch: main
3. Site will be available at `https://username.github.io/Sanchezforgovernor/`

No build step required. All files are static HTML/CSS.
